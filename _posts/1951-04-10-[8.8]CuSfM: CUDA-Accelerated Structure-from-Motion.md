# [8.8] CuSfM: CUDA-Accelerated Structure-from-Motion

- Authors: Jingrui Yu, Jun Liu, Kefei Ren, Joydeep Biswas, Rurui Ye, Keqiang Wu, Chirag Majithia, Di...
- [arXiv Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.15271v1)
- [PDF Link](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.15271v1.pdf)

## Subfields
 3D Reconstruction / Mapping & Localization
## Reason for Interest

This paper presents a highly practical and robust CUDA-accelerated SfM system tailored for autonomous driving mapping. 

1. **Relevance**: Direct application to AD HD Map creation, supporting multi-camera rigs, rolling shutter models, and extrinsic calibration.
2. **Innovation**: Combines deep learning features (ALIKED/LightGlue) with classical optimization on GPU. Proposes a 'view-graph' strategy based on pose priors to reduce redundant matching, solving a key bottleneck in large-scale mapping.
3. **Completeness**: Extensive experiments on KITTI and simulation data; compares against industry standards (COLMAP, GLOMAP). 
4. **Credibility**: Developed by NVIDIA, open-source code (PyCuSfM) enhances its potential impact on the industry. 

The system specifically targets the 'mapping with priors' setting common in AD, outperforming general-purpose SfM tools in both speed and robustness in this domain.
